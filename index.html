1. Executive Summary

Fankoin is a blockchain platform and ecosystem designed to power fan-driven economies in entertainment ‚Äî spanning music, film, sports, esports, television, and online creators.

Unlike traditional fan token systems that rely solely on fungible tokens, Fankoin Fan Tokens are fractionalized Digital Assets:

  • Each represents a unique, non-fungible digital asset (e.g., a special edition artwork, film scene, concert pass)
  • Fractionalized into ERC-compatible shards (FAN-FRAC standard) so multiple fans can co-own a high-value asset

2. Problem Statement

Traditional vs. Blockchain Fan Engagement

  • Traditional fan engagement fails to let audiences own a piece of the entertainment they love ‚Äî most monetization is transactional, not participatory.
  • Digital Asset markets have proven the power of digital collectability but lack easy fractional ownership for mainstream fans.
  • Existing token launchpads (e.g., pump.fun) make creation easy but prioritize speculation over sustainable, utility-backed assets.
  • Fan token ecosystems (e.g., Chiliz) focus mainly on sports and limit asset flexibility for creators in other entertainment sectors.

Core Asset Standards:

  • FAN: platform governance token (fungible)
  • Fan Tokens: Digital Asset-based tokens fractionalized via FAN-FRAC protocol, enabling co-ownership
  • Creator Assets: additional Digital Assets or semi-fungible tokens (ERC-1155 style) for tickets, merch rights, event passes, or media collectibles

Fractionalization Mechanism:

  1. Digital Asset is locked in a smart contract
  2. Fungible shards (fractions) are minted and distributed or sold to fans
  3. Fractions can be traded on the marketplace, staked for benefits, or redeemed collectively to unlock the underlying Digital Asset
